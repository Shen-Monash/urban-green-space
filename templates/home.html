Our maps can help you quickly find green spaces in Melbourne CBD. Our Plants page stores common local Melbourne plants that are suitable for planting. Getting Started with your plants

There are lots of other resources available online for people to explore, such as Gardening Australia, Better Homes and Gardens and
Gardening Australia Indoor Plant Series (https://www.abc.net.au/gardening/indoor-plants/12920308) Better Homes and Gardens (https://www.bhg.com.au/garden)
On these pages, you can learn a lot more about the best locations, watering habits, soils, and lighting for your plants, with blogs and videos to help you engage and visualise what your spaces can look like.
